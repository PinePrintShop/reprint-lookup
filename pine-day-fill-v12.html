Impressions = pieces (locations). Load = press-time-weighted impressions — sweatshirts & fleece (Sweatshirt, Pullover/Zip Hoodie, Youth Fleece) run slower, so each fleece piece counts ~1.35× toward a press-day budget (tunable). Cards show raw impressions plus a fleece tag; columns & day totals are in load. Work is grouped by platen family so a platen gets mounted once and the whole batch runs before swapping. Orders stay together; complex/large orders lean to P1 and only split across both presses the same day when they can't fit one. Hard Deadlines pack first and earliest (max lead time); any HD landing <3 days before its Customer Due Date is flagged tight, and after the date late — in both the current and suggested views. ASAP work is capped at a 15-business-day turnaround. Holidays (e.g. 7/3) are skipped. Recommend-only — nothing is written to Airtable.

Back room (the TAJIMA station): each day also shows embroidery (load = Run Time spindle-hours) and finishing (load = pieces ÷ throughput — Fold & Bag, UPC, Bandana Hanger). A + Helper flag appears on any week where embroidery exceeds the weekly trigger, finishing exceeds what one tech absorbs solo (solo h/day × 5), or a single finishing job tops the big-job threshold — all tunable in Settings. This week / From next Mon re-anchors the board to the current Mon–Fri or the next, in both views. Finishing imprints with no Scheduled Start show in the Finishing unscheduled count — give them a start date so they hit the queue.
